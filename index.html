Standard Deviation Tool

"The average distance from the average"

📝 The Formulas

Raw Data

$$\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum x^2}{n} - \bar{x}^2}$$

Grouped Data (with frequencies)

$$\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum fx^2}{n} - \bar{x}^2}$$

Variance ($\sigma^2$) is simply the value before you square root.

⚖️ Consistency

  • ✔ Small Standard Deviation ($\sigma$): Data is consistent and close to the mean.
  • ✘ Large Standard Deviation ($\sigma$): Data is spread out and inconsistent.
