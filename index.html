Standard Deviation Tool

"The average distance from the average"

📝 The Formula

For both Raw and Grouped data, we use:

$$\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum fx^2}{n} - \bar{x}^2}$$

Tip: "Mean of the squares minus square of the mean."

⚖️ Consistency

  • ✔ Small $\sigma$: Consistent, low spread, reliable.
  • ✘ Large $\sigma$: Inconsistent, high spread, unreliable.
